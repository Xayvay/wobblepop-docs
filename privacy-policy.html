Privacy Policy for Wobble Pop

Last Updated: January 2025

Introduction

Wobble Pop ("we," "our," or "us") is developed by Xavier Shelton, a solo developer committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile game application ("App").

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Player Names: If you choose to use the leaderboard feature, you may provide a player name (or use an anonymous name)
  • Game Progress: Your game scores, levels completed, and stars earned (stored locally on your device)

Information Collected Automatically

  • Game Statistics: Scores, levels, stars, and gameplay data (stored locally)
  • Device Information: Basic device information may be collected for app functionality

Information from Third-Party Services

If you choose to sign in using OAuth providers (Google, Facebook, Twitter, Discord), we may receive:

  • Your name (from the OAuth provider)
  • Your email address (if provided by the OAuth provider)
  • A unique identifier from the OAuth provider

Note: We do not store your email address or access your OAuth account details beyond what is necessary for leaderboard functionality.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide Game Services: Track your progress, scores, and game statistics
  • Leaderboard Functionality: Display your scores and rank on the leaderboard (if you opt-in)
  • Improve the App: Analyze gameplay data to improve game features and user experience
  • Customer Support: Respond to your inquiries and provide support

Data Storage

Local Storage

Game progress, scores, and settings are stored locally on your device using shared_preferences. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers unless you use the leaderboard feature.

Cloud Storage (Leaderboard Only)

If you use the leaderboard feature:

  • Your player name, scores, levels, and stars are stored in Supabase (our database provider)
  • This data is associated with a unique user ID (not your personal information)
  • You can use anonymous authentication, which does not require any personal information

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

Supabase

  • Purpose: Leaderboard and authentication services
  • Data: Player names, scores, levels, stars, and authentication tokens
  • Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy
  • Location: Data is stored in Supabase's secure servers

OAuth Providers (Optional)

  • Google, Facebook, Twitter, Discord: Used only for authentication if you choose to sign in
  • Data: We only receive your name and a unique identifier
  • Privacy Policies:
    • Google: https://policies.google.com/privacy
    • Facebook: https://www.facebook.com/privacy/explanation
    • Twitter: https://twitter.com/privacy
    • Discord: https://discord.com/privacy

Open Trivia Database

  • Purpose: Provides trivia questions for the game
  • Data: No personal data is shared with this service
  • License: CC BY-SA 4.0 (Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 4.0)
  • Privacy Policy: https://opentdb.com/api_config.php

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

  • Data encryption in transit (HTTPS)
  • Secure authentication through Supabase
  • Row-level security policies to prevent unauthorized access
  • Input validation and sanitization to prevent abuse

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request access to your personal data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Opt-Out: Stop using the leaderboard feature at any time (data will remain but won't be updated)

To exercise these rights, contact us at: xayvair@gmail.com

Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Data Retention

  • Local Data: Stored on your device until you uninstall the app or clear app data
  • Leaderboard Data: Retained as long as your account is active or as required by law

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
